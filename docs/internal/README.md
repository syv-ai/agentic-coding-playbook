# Internal docs

Working notes, planning documents, and editorial decisions for the playbook. Not part of the published workshop material.

The published modules live one level up in `docs/` (00–14). Anything in this folder is for contributors deciding *what* to write, not learners reading *what was written*.

## What belongs here

- Coverage plans for open issues
- Editorial decisions and their rationale
- Track / structure proposals before they're settled
- Drafts and outlines that aren't ready to land in a numbered module
- Style decisions (visual language, terminology) once they exist

## What does not belong here

- Anything a workshop participant should read — that goes in `docs/`
- Research notes that already have a home in `docs/research-summary.md`
